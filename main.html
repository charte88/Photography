About Me

My name is Cameron. I was born in Sacramento and currently live in the beautiful city of San Francisco. I started my journey as a photographer by taking a class at a community college using a Canon AE1 film camera and discovered a love for landscape and street photography. Two years later, I traveled to Tokyo for some great street photography, lived in Washington to capture the breathtaking nature, and now I am in San Francisco in hopes of expanding my photography skills.

When I am not at school working on my Computer Science degree, you will find me capturing what makes this world so unique.

Please stay tuned for more pictures and future projects to be added!
